About Weather Graph

I used to use a specific weather app on my phone because I liked its graph. However, its server was down just enough to really annoy me. I tried a dozen other apps looking for a similar graph, but couldn't find one. So I made my own.

3rd Party APIs

Weather Graph does not do any forecasting itself. Instead it gathers data from other sources to display in the graph. Those sources are:

OpenWeather: https://openweathermap.org/

Visual Crossing https://www.visualcrossing.com/

Weather.gov: https://www.weather.gov/

Weather Unlocked: https://developer.weatherunlocked.com/

In addition, Weather Graph uses Location IQ when you search for a custom location, and to determine city names and time zones:

Location IQ: http://locationapi.org/
