Privacy Policy

This Statement of Privacy applies to https://weather.simonton.app and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Weather Graph include https://weather.simonton.app. By using Weather Graph, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. This happens when you grant Weather Graph permission to access your location via your web browser, or when manually entering your location into the "Custom" field within Weather Graph.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

Weather Graph does not sell, rent or lease personal information to third parties.

Weather Graph may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Weather Graph, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Weather Graph may disclose your personal information, without notice, if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Weather Graph; and/or (b) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Weather Graph, or the public.

Tracking User Behavior

Weather Graph may keep track of the features our users use within Weather Graph, in order to determine which are the most popular.

We use Mixpanel in this process. We allow Mixpanel to collect your location no more fine-grained than your city. It does not store your IP address. We do not send Mixpanel any personally identifying information, nor do we let Mixpanel store any cookies for Weather Graph.

Automatically Collected Information

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Weather Graph. This information can include: your browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Weather Graph website.

Security of your Personal Information

Weather Graph secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. When personal information is transmitted to other services, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol.

We strive to take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or alteration of your personal information. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your personal information, you acknowledge that: (a) there are security and privacy limitations inherent to the Internet which are beyond our control; and (b) security, integrity, and privacy of any and all information and data exchanged between you and us through this Site cannot be guaranteed.

Children Under Thirteen

Weather Graph does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If you are under the age of thirteen, you must ask your parent or guardian for permission to use this website.

External Data Storage Sites

We may store your data on servers provided by third party hosting vendors with whom we have contracted.
